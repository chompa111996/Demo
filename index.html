About Oakridge Academy

Established in 1995, Oakridge Academy has been committed to providing exceptional education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, creativity, and character development.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students to become lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and responsible global citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading educational institution that inspires excellence, fosters innovation, and cultivates compassionate leaders prepared to address the challenges of tomorrow.

Admissions

Join our vibrant learning community. We welcome applications from students who are eager to learn, grow, and contribute to our school community.

Admission Process

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form with all required documents.

2

Assessment

Students complete grade-appropriate assessments to determine academic readiness.

3

Interview

Families meet with our admissions team to discuss educational goals and expectations.

4

Decision

Admission decisions are communicated within 2-3 weeks after completing all steps.
